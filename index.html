Common questions

What happens after my 2-month free trial?
After 2 months you can choose any paid plan — Basic starts at just $1.99/mo. If you don't pick a plan, your account moves to read-only mode so you never lose your saved plans.
Do I need an Anthropic API key?
Nope — on all paid plans Pantreo handles the AI behind the scenes. No API keys, no extra costs, no technical setup.
How is Pantreo different from Mealime or PlateJoy?
They use fixed recipe databases — you'll eventually see repeats. Pantreo's AI generates a completely unique plan every time, for any dietary combo, at a lower price than both.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — no contracts, no hassle. Cancel from your account settings and keep access until the end of your billing period.
How do I get support?
Email us anytime at pantreo.support@gmail.com — we typically respond within 24 hours. Max plan users get priority responses.
What's the annual discount?
Pay yearly and save 35% across all plans. Basic drops to $1.29/mo, Pro to $5.19/mo, and Max to $8.45/mo.
